Tribute to Rakesh Sharma

Rakesh Sharma

Rakesh Sharma is an Indian astronaut who made history by becoming the first Indian citizen to travel in space. He was born on January 13, 1949, in Patiala, Punjab, India.

On April 2, 1984, Rakesh Sharma embarked on his historic journey aboard the Soyuz T-11 spacecraft, spending 8 days, 21 hours, and 40 minutes in space. During his mission, he famously responded to then-Prime Minister Indira Gandhi's question about how India looked from space with the iconic words,"Saare Jahan Se Achha" (Better than the entire world).

Rakesh Sharma's contribution to space exploration has left an indelible mark on India's space program, and he continues to inspire generations of aspiring astronauts.
